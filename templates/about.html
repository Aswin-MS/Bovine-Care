About Bovine Care

Bovine Care is a revolutionary web application designed to assist cattle farmers in detecting and managing diseases like lumpy skin and ringworm. By leveraging advanced image analysis and AI-driven diagnostics, we empower farmers with the tools to safeguard their livestock's health.

Our mission:

"Our mission is to provide farmers with an easy-to-use, technology-driven solution to detect cattle diseases early, facilitate communication among farmers, and connect them with veterinary experts for better animal healthcare."

Key Features

AI-Powered Disease Detection:

Upload images or videos of cattle to detect diseases like lumpy skin and ringworm using AI.

Farmer Communication Forum:

Engage in discussions with fellow farmers through a dedicated chat interface.

Expert Veterinary Assistance:

Farmers can ask questions and receive guidance from veterinary professionals.

Secure Login:

Separate dashboards for farmers and doctors for personalized assistance.

Why Choose Bovine Care?

Accurate & Fast Diagnosis.
